About Our School

ZPHS Munigadapa is a government high school located in Munigadapa village, Jagadevpur Mandal, Siddipet District, Telangana. Established around 1950, the school offers education from grades 6 to 10 and serves as a pillar of learning in the rural community. The school is part of the Telangana governmentâ€™s "Mana Ooru Mana Badi" program to upgrade infrastructure and learning facilities.

The school has over 280 students and 16 teachers, offering quality education and values. With well-built classrooms, a library, a generator, and mid-day meals, the school strives to provide a holistic environment for learning.
